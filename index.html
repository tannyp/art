Tanjot Panesar is a movement director who builds work from a foundation of mindfulness and presence. They have a unique gift for turning colors, moods, and abstract ideas into carefully chosen movements and positions that bring concepts to life. Tanjot’s style is raw, bold, and emotionally charged—often using unusual shapes and intense physicality to stir something deep in the viewer. Their process blends stillness with chaos, creating movement that feels both powerful and human, always aiming to tell a story that words alone can’t express.

BASED IN NYC.

Emotion into motion. Creating spaces where choreography and orchestration fades and only spontaneity remains. This is the mission.

Currently, caputuring meaningful stories through film and building a community of mindful movers.





Beginnings
Trained in multiple movement styles such as hip-hop, street-styles, qi-gong & contemporary. He has developed a unique and holistic way of storytelling that is driven by incorporating breathwork, visualization, and method acting.
