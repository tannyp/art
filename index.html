Tanjot Panesar is a Punjabi-Canadian movement artist known for his innovation and storytelling abilities in choreography.

BASED IN TORONTO, CA.

The mission is to provide authenthic experiences within creative spaces - through teaching, designing and coaching movement. Oh and to bring some pretty kick-ass people together.

Currently, caputuring meaningful stories through film and building community.





Beginnings
Using his training in multiple movememnt styles such as hip-hop, street-styles, qi-gong & contemporary, he has developed a unique and holistic way of storytelling that is driven by incorporating breathwork, visualization, and method acting.
Experimenting
Experiencing new creative spaces, music and lifestyles around the world.
Previously
Co-creating the “forest” element in an interpretive live stage show about the deterioration of planet earth. Show by Kinaj, Shameka Blake.
